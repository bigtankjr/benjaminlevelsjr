About

  • 2008-2013

    What Started It All.

    While touring part-time and writing music, my job at General Motors went through a major restructuring. This brought into question the stability of working for the company. Without a solid education that takes into account an ever evolving future, how could I survive the ups and downs of the auto industry.

  • January 2014

    Hitting the Road

    I decided to make a decision that would change my life forever. I left the auto industry and moved to Los Angeles California.

  • June 2014

    Enrolled in Kent State's Computer Technology Program

    In the summer of 2014 I went back to school to begin my journey toward a Bachelor's degree in Computer Technology with a concentration in Application Development.

  • February 2015

    Started Working as a Programmer Analyst

    I began working for a Tech company in Inglewood California as a Programmer Analyst and following my passion for music part-time.

  • May 2017

    Graduation

    In May of 2017, I will finish my Bachelor's degree and continue a career in Software Development and Music Production; as well as, nurture current and new relationships with actors, artists, and entrepreneurs in the Los Angeles area.
